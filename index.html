Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I forgot to clock out?

Our system automatically clocks you out at 5:00 PM on the day you clocked in. For any further corrections, please contact your manager.

Q: I clicked the wrong button. How do I fix it?

Only managers can edit or delete time logs. Please message your manager as soon as possible so they can correct the mistake on your behalf.

Q: Can I check my total hours for the week?

This portal only shows your current shift status. Your manager tracks your total weekly hours through the Manager Dashboard and handles all payroll calculations.

Q: Why is my Clock In or Clock Out button disabled?

The system prevents double-punching. If you are already clocked in, you can only clock out — and vice versa. This keeps your timesheet accurate and prevents duplicate records.
